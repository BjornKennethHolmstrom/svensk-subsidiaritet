# Proposals

Five structural problems where the system's stated goal and actual incentives point in opposite directions. Five mechanisms that reverse the incentives — without requiring more resources.

All proposals are free to use, adapt, and implement.

## The Pattern

All five proposals do the same thing: they identify a place where the system's stated goal and actual incentives point in opposite directions, and offer a mechanism that reverses the incentives without requiring more resources.

| Problem | System's Goal | Actual Incentive | The Mechanism |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Housing | Cheap housing for all | Maximize price per m² | Trade density for CLT land |
| Resilience | Psychological defense | Ignore everyday stress | Use existing infrastructure |
| Loneliness | Social cohesion | Cheaper to isolate | Create currency for contact |
| Rural Areas | The whole of Sweden should live | Extract value, export people | Tax what stays |
| Security | Protect against risk | Trap in passivity | Separate floor and insurance |
