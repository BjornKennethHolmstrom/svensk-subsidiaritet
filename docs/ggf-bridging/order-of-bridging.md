### Implementation Guidance for Each Analysis

Following the pattern established in `valfard.md`, each document should follow the structure:

1. **Cybernetisk Analys** (Ashby's Law framing of the specific sector failure)
2. **Aktiva Systemfel** (Concrete examples: *Algoritmisk tyranni* equivalent)
3. **Subsidiaritetsbaserade Åtgärder** (Specific local pilots)
4. **Verktygslådan** (The GGF Bridge Section—this is the critical new addition)

For **Äldreomsorg** (the next one), the *Verktygslådan* section should explicitly introduce:

- **Hearts as "Omsorgspoäng"**: A local currency earned by *anhörigvårdare* (family carers) and retirees helping each other, redeemable for *avlastning* (respite care) or a discount on *hemtjänstavgift*.
- **Community Provider Charter**: How a local *pensionärsförening* (pensioners' association) can become a formal care provider without the full bureaucratic burden of a licensed nursing home.
- **The 15-Hour Cap**: Aligning with *Work in Liberation* principles to ensure that volunteering doesn't become unpaid labor exploitation.

### A Note on Pacing
I recommend publishing these at a steady rhythm (e.g., one every two weeks) to allow the Swedish institutional ecosystem (SKR, *Dagens Samhälle*, *Folkrörelseforum*) to digest and respond to each piece. By the time you hit **Energi**, you'll have a solid coalition of care actors and local food advocates ready to defend the vision when you challenge the more entrenched interests of energy utilities and housing developers.
