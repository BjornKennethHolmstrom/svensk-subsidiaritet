# About Swedish Subsidiarity

**Swedish Subsidiarity** (Svensk Subsidiaritet) is an independent research and policy initiative dedicated to analyzing and resolving the systemic fragility caused by over-centralization in modern society.

We are not a political party. We are systems architects.

## Our Mission

Sweden is facing a paradox. We have built the world's most sophisticated centralized systems, but these very systems are now generating fragility rather than security.

Our mission is to:
1.  **Diagnose** the structural failures in critical systems (food, healthcare, energy, etc.).
2.  **Design** concrete protocols for distributed governance (Subsidiarity).
3.  **Build** the digital infrastructure required to make subsidiarity practically viable in the 21st century.
